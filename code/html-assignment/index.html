ATLAS

A story of trust.

Everyone needs someone in their life, satisfying the quote: No man is an island.

Atlas, a movie propagating artificial intelligence gone rogue, is a story about a girl who was betrayed by AI which she trusted and to defeat it, in order to save humanity, she has to trust AI again.

What a turn out of events!

Are you intrigued by this pages info? Then go watch the movie...

Atlas Trailer

Lessons learned from the movie:

  1. We all need a connection with someone.
  2. Our past betrayals don't determine our future connections.
  3. When 2 people trust each other there's no telling what they can do together. It's beyond comprehension!

The real question is: Will AI ever get to the point of controlling and destroying humanity like disseminated by the movie?

It's not the only movie spreading this idea. There is also Margaux amongst many others!
