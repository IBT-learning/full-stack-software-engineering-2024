Interesting Natural and Tourist Sites

Riyom LGA Plateau State,Nigeria

About:Riyom is a Local Government Area in Plateau State, Nigeria. Its headquarters are in the town of Riyom to the north of the Area at 9°38′00″N 8°46′00″E.It has an area of 807 km² and a population of 131,557 at the 2006 census, which is predominantly Berom. The LGA has boundaries with Kaduna and Nasarawa State. It is the gateway to the State when coming from the East and from Abuja.

Top three natural attractive sites

      Assop Falls

    One of the most notable of Nigeria's many waterfalls. Located at the edge of the Jos Plateau, about 40 miles (64 km) from Jos city, on the road to Abuja Federal Capital Nigeria. It is used as a filming location for soap operas and advertisements.

      Kahwhang Basal Rock Formation

    Basalt rocks, is located in Bangai village in the Bachi district of Riyom.Formed by erupting basalt lava during solidification as a result of columnar jointing

      Riyom Rock

    Located 25km southwest from Jos and 75km from Abuja FCT, is no doubt one of the most visited landscape in Nigeria,known locally as "Beta Betat" meaning three stones
