UNEMPLOYMENT IN KENYA

The impact of Unemployment

Unemployment is hitting globally , with Kenya being the most affected. The current reality is that it has caused high crime rates ,a strain on public finances,reduced consumer spending,increased dependence on social welfare programs, reduced social cohesion and overall low living standards. Effects of unemployment

THE REALITY

Unemployment has greatly hit Kenyans between the age of 18-65 years, making it hard to support their basic needs and those of their families. They are unable to afford meals, shelter , healthcare services , school fees and some Individuals get involved in drug and substance abuse and other engages in criminal activities as they try to survive. Kenyas's unemployment rate

FACTORS CONTRIBUTING TO UNEMPLOYMENT

  1. Economic factors such as undeveloped labor markets.
  2. High labor supply and low labor demand.
  3. Government policies such as minimum wage floors and generous social benefits programs
