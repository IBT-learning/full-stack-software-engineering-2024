My Lagos Paradise

Hello! Welcome to my Lagos Paradise. I like to chill out, relax and go places. So, I seek out spots for adventure-seeking, wander-lusting souls like myself. I just discovered this new haven of paradise, so tag along and enjoy the ride!

Hello Lakowe Golf Resort and Country Estates!

Lagos is the second-largest city in Africa. With a population of just under 15 million people, Lagosians never sleep or really take time off to rest. Several factors are responsible for this, ranging from the spiralling inflation, never ending traffic, and the boundless energy of the people who live here! In this city, you can never really find the right kind of place to relax and unwind, or so I thought, until I stumbled on Lakowe Golf Resort.

Lakowe Golf Resort is a picturesque estate with a lovely golf course built around a lake. It is the rendezvous for anyone itching for a getaway in the bustling Lagos City. Located 7.3 km from Lekki Conservation Centre, Lakowe Lakes Golf Resort and Country Estate gives you a taste of luxury with its scenic 18-Hole Golf Course, cozy cottages, tasty menus, and plenty of water sports to keep you entertained, fit and very relaxed!

Here are some fun things to do at Lakowe Golf Resort

  • Play golf
  • Boat Rides
  • Bonfire Nights
  • long walks around the lakes and scenic gardens
  • Outdoor dining
  • Cycling
